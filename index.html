ABOUT US

NivaCrafts is the sister concern of Hobb Creation Industries which was established 2008 with the mission to promote Nepalese Felt Industries Worldwide.

We have a total staff of more than 200 who are busy in the manufacture of the new handmade samples and products to be supplied worldwide. Our main motto is promoting Nepalese Handmade Products worldwide using the local skilled household women. Our Annual turnover is more than 1 million.
